<p><p>The Magnetic Field Meter market comprises several types based on different sensing technologies. Hall Effect Magnetic Field Meters are widely used for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Magnetoresistive meters, including AMR, GMR, and TMR types, offer high sensitivity and wide measurement ranges. MEMS-based meters leverage micro-electromechanical systems for compact designs. SQUID meters excel in detecting weak magnetic fields due to their extreme sensitivity, while Fluxgate meters are known for precision in measuring low-frequency magnetic fields, making them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
