<p><ul><li>Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers & ICs</li><li>Powered Device Controllers & ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) solutions market includes two primary segments: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) controllers and integrated circuits (ICs), and Powered Device (PD) controllers and ICs. PSE controllers manage power delivery to devices over Ethernet, ensuring efficient energy transfer and power allocation. In contrast, PD controllers and ICs are designed for devices that receive power, facilitating power management and communication. Together, these components enable seamless integration of power and data, enhancing network infrastructure and supporting a wide range of applications.</p></p>
