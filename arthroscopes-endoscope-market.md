<p><p>The arthroscopes endoscope market encompasses three primary types: straight, semi-flexible, and bent arthroscopes. Straight arthroscopes provide a rigid structure, ideal for accessing joint spaces in a linear fashion. Semi-flexible arthroscopes combine rigidity with some degree of flexibility, allowing for better maneuverability in complex anatomical areas. Bent arthroscopes feature angled tips that enable access to challenging spots within the joint, facilitating a more comprehensive examination and treatment. Each type caters to specific procedural needs, enhancing precision in minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
