<p><p>Handheld optical attenuators are devices used to reduce the power of an optical signal in fiber optic networks, ensuring accurate testing and measurement. In the market, there are two primary types: single mode and multimode attenuators. Single mode attenuators are designed for single mode fibers, which carry light over long distances with minimal loss. Multimode attenuators cater to multimode fibers, which support multiple light modes over shorter distances, often used in local area networks. Each type serves distinct applications based on fiber characteristics.</p></p>
