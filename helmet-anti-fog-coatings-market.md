<p><ul><li>Hydrophilic Coating</li><li>Nanocoating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The helmet anti-fog coatings market includes primarily two types: hydrophilic coatings and nanocoatings. Hydrophilic coatings work by spreading moisture evenly across the surface, preventing fog from forming. These coatings absorb water vapor and minimize visibility issues. In contrast, nanocoatings utilize nanotechnology to create extremely thin layers that repel water, thus maintaining clarity. Both types enhance safety and performance in various activities such as sports, industrial work, and motorcycling, catering to the growing demand for effective anti-fog solutions.</p></p>
