<p><p>Hybrid scrubbers are advanced systems designed to reduce pollutant emissions from maritime vessels, combining both open-loop and closed-loop operations. As stricter sulfur emission limits come into force, more shipping companies are investing in hybrid technologies to mitigate regulatory risks and improve their environmental footprint. The transition towards low-sulfur fuels and the pressure to meet decarbonization targets are further accelerating the adoption of these systems.</p><p>Recent trends in the hybrid scrubbers market include technological advancements that enhance efficiency and effectiveness, as well as a rise in retrofit installations.
