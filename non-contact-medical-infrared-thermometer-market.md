<p><p>The non-contact medical infrared thermometer market comprises primarily two types: forehead and ear thermometers. Forehead type thermometers measure surface temperature from a distance, providing quick readings without skin contact, ideal for screening and sensitive populations. Ear type thermometers use infrared technology to measure temperature inside the ear canal, offering precise core body temperature readings. Both types are essential for minimizing cross-contamination, ensuring safety, and providing efficient temperature assessments in healthcare settings and at home.</p></p>
