<p><p>The electro-hydraulic press market is categorized into two primary types: horizontal and vertical electro-hydraulic presses. Horizontal electro-hydraulic presses are designed for operations requiring wide workspace and allow for easier loading and unloading of materials, making them ideal for large-scale manufacturing. In contrast, vertical electro-hydraulic presses occupy less floor space and are suited for applications requiring greater force in a compact format, often used in deep drawing and forming processes. Both types leverage hydraulic power for efficiency and precision.</p></p>
