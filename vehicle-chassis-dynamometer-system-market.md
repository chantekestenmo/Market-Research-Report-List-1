<p><p>The Vehicle Chassis Dynamometer System market comprises two main types: Multi Roller and Single Roller systems. Multi Roller dynamometers utilize multiple rollers to simulate various driving conditions, allowing for more accurate testing of vehicle performance, including power and emissions under different load scenarios. In contrast, Single Roller systems feature a single roller design, which is generally simpler and more cost-effective, making them suitable for basic assessments. Both types are crucial for testing and calibrating vehicles in automotive research and development.</p></p>
